## 🔍 Overview

**NexaShop** is an AI-powered ecommerce platform designed to maximize conversions for online stores. It combines neural product recommendations, ultra-fast edge delivery, and automated dynamic pricing into a single seamless platform — helping sellers turn more browsers into buyers, automatically.

> ⚡ **200ms** avg load time · 🛒 **3.8×** conversion lift · 📦 **12M+** products indexed · 🏪 **4,200+** brands

---

## ✨ Platform Features

### 🧠 Neural Recommendations
A transformer model that reads every browse signal, cart action, and purchase history in real-time. It surfaces exactly what each user wants before they even search. Average basket size up **42%**.

### ⚡ Edge Commerce Delivery
Storefront served from **200+ global edge nodes**. Sub-200ms loads anywhere. No cold starts, no spinners — just instant product pages that convert at 3× industry average.

### 🎯 Dynamic Pricing Engine
Real-time demand sensing + competitor price monitoring + margin-aware automation. Prices always right, always competitive, always maximising revenue — zero manual rules needed.
